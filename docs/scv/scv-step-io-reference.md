## Notes for Incident Work

- The shard-side raw unit files under `/var/opt/nio/aggregations/scv_matches/YYYY-MM-DD/` are the key retention-sensitive inputs for rebuilding a date from the shard.
- The master-side partials under `/var/opt/nio/aggregations/scv/YYYY-MM-DD/` show which shard contributions actually reached scv_master.
- `home_analytics.YYYY-MM-DD.log` is usually the first output checked when QA reports a drop in red or blue devices.
- Missing `scv.domain_report` or `scv.raw_matches_report` does not automatically mean `home_analytics` is wrong; reporting and core SCV should be checked separately.
